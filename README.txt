Most Zigbee double sockets appear in Homey as a single device, leaving you no only able to turn the left socket on and off. This app changes that by giving each socket its own independent on/off control, so you can manage them exactly as you would two separate smart plugs.

Currently supported devices are the Aurora Aone AU-A1ZBDSS and the Scolmore Click Smart+ double socket. More devices may be added as they are tested and confirmed to work.
